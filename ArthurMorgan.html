Cowboy

For this process I downloaded and printed a stl of Arthur Morgan from Red Dead Redemption 2. For the base I used a part of a bourbon barrel head. I then zenithal primed the models and I would proceed to paint with diluted paint so it shows off the zenithal priming. I tried to make the base a desert orange so that it can contrast with the blue of Arthur's shirt.
